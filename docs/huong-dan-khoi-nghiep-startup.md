### 3.1. MVP Types v√† When to Use

**Landing Page MVP**
- **When**: Test demand before building
- **Tools**: Unbounce, WordPress, Webflow
- **Metrics**: Email signups, conversion rates

**Concierge MVP** 
- **When**: Service-based businesses
- **Approach**: Manually deliver service to validate concept
- **Example**: Food delivery founder personally delivering orders

**Wizard of Oz MVP**
- **When**: Complex backend can be simulated manually
- **Approach**: Frontend looks automated, backend is manual
- **Example**: Chatbot that's actually human operators

**Feature MVP**
- **When**: Adding new functionality to existing product
- **Approach**: Build minimal version of feature
- **Example**: Instagram Stories (copy of Snapchat Stories)

### 3.3. MVP Development Process

## B∆∞·ªõc 7: Legal v√† Regulatory Compliance

### 7.1. Business Registration Process

**Step 1: Choose Business Structure**
- **Limited Liability Company (LLC)**: Most common, flexible
- **Joint Stock Company**: For raising capital, going public eventually
- **Representative Office**: For foreign companies testing market

**Step 2: Registration Process**
1. **Name reservation** (1-3 days)
2. **Submit application** to Department of Planning and Investment
3. **Receive Enterprise Registration Certificate** (15 days)
4. **Tax registration** (automatically done)
5. **Seal registration** (1-2 days)

**Required Capital:**
- **LLC**: Minimum VND 10 million (~$400)
- **JSC**: No minimum but must declare charter capital

### 7.2. Intellectual Property Protection

**Trademark Registration:**
- **Cost**: VND 1.8-3.6 million (~$75-150)
- **Timeline**: 12-18 months
- **Validity**: 10 years, renewable

**Patent Filing:**
- **Invention patent**: 20 years protection
- **Utility solution**: 10 years protection  
- **Industrial design**: 5-15 years protection

**Copyright Protection:**
- **Automatic**: No registration required
- **Optional registration**: For stronger legal protection
- **Software**: Protect source code v√† algorithms

### 7.3. Tax Obligations

**Corporate Income Tax (CIT):**
- **Standard rate**: 20%
- **Preferential rates**: 10-17% for certain industries/locations
- **New tech companies**: Potential 10% rate for 4 years

**Value Added Tax (VAT):**
- **Standard rate**: 10%  
- **Reduced rates**: 5% for certain goods/services
- **Export services**: 0% VAT

**Personal Income Tax** (for employees):
- **Progressive rates**: 5% - 35%
- **Monthly threshold**: VND 11 million tax-free
